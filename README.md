### Trojan Galactic
- Trojan Galactic is a sci-fi stealth game that immerses you in a slow desolated off-world mining colony known as “The Morgue”. As an engineer, you navigate through a facility guided by a voice over an intercom that claims to be on your side. As you explore the facility and uncover information, you’ll slowly discover you are part of an AI experiment gone rogue.
- [TrojanGalactic](https://reflexxak.itch.io/trojan-galactic)
